Strengthening Ties: Key Meeting with Pag-IBIG Davao Marks Progress on KC Homes Subdivision

On July 10, 2025, Landlan Realty and Development Corp. reached an important milestone for KC Homes Subdivision through a productive meeting with the Pag-IBIG Fund team from Davao City. Held at Landlan Office – Cotabato Branch, the meeting was led by Sir Broi M. Barba, Pag-IBIG Fund Accounts Analyst, and Ma'am Kathryn Anne Racoma, Housing Relationship Officer. The agenda focused on two key areas: Pag-IBIG Accreditation and the End-User Housing Loan Program.



For the accreditation, the Pag-IBIG team walked us through the process and highlighted two main categories of requirements: project requirements and developer’s requirements. Fulfilling these is essential to becoming an accredited developer, which will enable our buyers to access Pag-IBIG financing options.
The second part of the meeting centered on the End-User Housing Loan Program. Pag-IBIG emphasized the importance of each buyer's capacity to pay and outlined the complete set of documents buyers must submit. A key takeaway from this discussion was the update on the new price threshold that Pag-IBIG now uses for socialized housing, which will help us ensure our pricing remains aligned with financing qualifications and affordable housing policies.



After the meeting, the Pag-IBIG Davao team, together with representatives from the Ministry of Human Settlements and Development (MHSD - BARMM), visited the KC Homes Subdivision site. They inspected both the location and our model house, offering valuable insights and support as we move toward accreditation and loan takeout preparation.



This collaboration marks a significant milestone for KC Homes Subdivision and reflects our continued commitment to delivering affordable, accessible housing solutions. We thank Pag-IBIG Davao and MHSD-BARMM for their time, guidance, and dedication to helping us serve our future homeowners better.
